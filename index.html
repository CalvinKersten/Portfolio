Calvin Kersten

Hey there, I'm Calvin Kersten, and I'm all about embracing life's creative and adventurous side. I wear many hats, and my passions span a wide spectrum of exciting activities. First and foremost, my guitar is my constant companion. Strumming those strings and creating soulful melodies is not just a hobby; it's a way of expressing my inner self. But I don't stop at music. Photography is another love of mine. Capturing the world's beauty through the lens of my camera is a never-ending quest for me. Every click is a chance to freeze a moment in time and share its magic with the world. Then there's filmmaking and scriptwriting. I'm a storyteller at heart, and the world of cinema allows me to bring those stories to life. Crafting scripts and turning them into visual masterpieces is a thrilling journey I'm always eager to embark upon. And for that rush of adrenaline and the joy of cruising through life, I turn to longboarding. There's something liberating about carving through the streets on a longboard, feeling the wind in my hair and the world beneath my feet. So, if you're ready for a ride through a world of music, art, and adventure, you're in the right place. Join me in exploring the dynamic and captivating life of Calvin Kersten, where artistry and exhilaration harmoniously coexist.
